Hosting

Our hoster collects the following data transmitted to or from your browser in server log files:

  • IP address,
  • the address of the previously visited website (referer request header),
  • date and time of the request,
  • time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time,
  • content of the request,
  • HTTP status code,
  • amount of data transferred,
  • website from which the request came, and
  • information about the browser and operating system.

This is necessary to display our website and to ensure stability and security. This corresponds to our legitimate interest in the sense of Art. 6 para. 1 p. 1 lit. f GDPR.

There is no tracking and we do not have direct access to these data, but only receive an anonymised statistical summary. This includes the address of the previously visited page, the frequency of the pages called up in each case and the number of unique visitors. We do not combine these data with other data.
